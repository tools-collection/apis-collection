# [Open Food Facts](https://en.wiki.openfoodfacts.org/API)


Open Food Facts is a database of food products with ingredients, allergens, nutrition facts and all the tidbits of information we can find on product labels.

## Details
- **Categories**: Food &amp; Drinks, Sport &amp; Fitness, Statistics &amp; Data
- **Type**: REST
- **Auth**: `none`
- **Free**: Yes 🆓
- **Status**: Graveyard 🪦
